ABOUT US

Direct Line Service, 100% customer focused

Direct Line Service is an American Company that is now proudly building their machinery in the United States of America. Not only is the "Made in America" quality standard important to us, but it is important to our clients and maintaining a strong, long standing, line operation.
Along with supporting our American made machines we also support many European brands such as; Solidtec, ProPak, Manter, Verbruggen, Symach, PIM, Newtec, C-pack, Inno-Tec, and many more...

Over 40 years experience went into us being able to offer an American made High speed computerized weighing machine. Which is utilized for a variety of produce lines, including Onions,Chalets, Potatoes, Walnuts, Almonds, Hay products, Carrots, etc.. With each install we provide custom layouts of your facility that will best utilize the available space and the functionality of the machines. Making sure that your facility is operating at its fullest capacity.

Our company goal is to provide our clients with the best possible machines for their job, and eliminating unnecessary labor costs in the process. With rising labor costs, our machinery is a valid solution. If upgrading isn't an option at the moment, our knowledgable Parts and Service department can provide you with the parts and labor to keep your current machines running.
